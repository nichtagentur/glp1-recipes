ingredients:
  - "4 boneless skinless chicken breasts (6 oz each)"
  - "2 lemons, juiced and zested"
  - "3 cloves garlic, minced"
  - "2 tbsp olive oil"
  - "1 cup quinoa, rinsed"
  - "2 cups low-sodium chicken broth"
  - "1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped"
  - "2 tbsp fresh oregano, chopped"
  - "Salt and pepper to taste"
instructions:
  - "Marinate chicken in lemon juice, zest, garlic, olive oil, and half the herbs for at least 15 minutes."
  - "Cook quinoa in chicken broth according to package directions. Fluff with a fork."
  - "Preheat grill or grill pan to medium-high heat."
  - "Grill chicken for 5-6 minutes per side until internal temperature reaches 165F."
  - "Let chicken rest for 5 minutes, then slice."
  - "Serve sliced chicken over quinoa, topped with remaining fresh herbs and a squeeze of lemon."
