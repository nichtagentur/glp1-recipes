ingredients:
  - "3 cups plain nonfat Greek yogurt (2% or 0% fat)"
  - "1 scoop (30g) unflavored or vanilla whey protein powder"
  - "1 cup fresh blueberries"
  - "1 cup fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced"
  - "1/2 cup fresh raspberries"
  - "4 tablespoons chia seeds"
  - "4 tablespoons natural almond butter"
  - "2 tablespoons raw honey or pure maple syrup"
  - "4 tablespoons low-sugar granola"
  - "1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract"
  - "1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon"
  - "Pinch of sea salt"
instructions:
  - "Add the plain Greek yogurt to a medium mixing bowl along with the vanilla whey protein powder, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and a pinch of sea salt."
  - "Stir the yogurt mixture vigorously with a spoon or whisk until the protein powder is fully incorporated and the mixture is smooth and creamy with no lumps remaining."
  - "Divide the yogurt base evenly among four serving bowls, using approximately 3/4 cup of the mixture per bowl."
  - "Top each bowl with 1/4 cup of fresh blueberries, 1/4 cup of sliced strawberries, and 2 tablespoons of fresh raspberries, arranging them in distinct sections for visual appeal."
  - "Drizzle 1 tablespoon of natural almond butter over the fruit in each bowl, allowing it to lightly pool between the berries."
  - "Sprinkle 1 tablespoon of chia seeds evenly over each bowl to add texture and a boost of fiber and omega-3 fatty acids."
  - "Add 1 tablespoon of low-sugar granola to each bowl for a satisfying crunch."
  - "Finish each bowl with a light drizzle of raw honey or maple syrup (approximately 1/2 tablespoon per bowl) and serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for up to 24 hours for a make-ahead breakfast option."

This Greek Yogurt Protein Bowl is the ultimate GLP-1-friendly breakfast, delivering 30 grams of protein per serving to maximize satiety hormones and keep hunger at bay for hours. The combination of high-fiber berries, chia seeds, and satiating almond butter works synergistically to support healthy digestion and steady blood sugar levels. Ready in just 10 minutes, this no-cook bowl is as nourishing as it is delicious, making it the perfect way to start your day with intention.
